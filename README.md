# Aeromedical-nexus-EMTECH-SIR-LJ-Website-Project-
AeroMedical Nexus is a student website showcasing our future careers in aviation, medicine, and space. It highlights the connection of aeronautics, healthcare, and technology in real-life applications like air ambulances and aerospace medicine. It includes About Me, Detailed Career, My Future Self, and a Gallery of dream career images.
